mysql命令行添加用户:

CREATE USER 'username'@'hostname' IDENTIFIED BY 'password' WITH GRANT OPTION;

给用户添加所有权限:

GRANT ALL ON *.* TO 'username'@'hostname';

撤销某个用户的权限

EVOKE ALL  ON dbName.tbName  FROM 'username'@'hostname';

查看某个用户的权限详情:

show grants for 'username'@'hostname';

查看mysql的字符集:

show variables like 'character_set_%';
